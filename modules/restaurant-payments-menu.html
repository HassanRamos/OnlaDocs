Restaurant Payments Menu

Review payment records, payment methods, order numbers, transaction dates, and pending balances.

What this module is forMain featuresTypical workflowGood practiceScreens / Visual Guide

What this module is for

  • The payments menu helps the restaurant monitor money received from customers.
  • It shows payment-related information linked to orders and helps identify unpaid or partially paid bills.

Main features

  • View payment records.
  • See payment method, order number, date, and time.
  • Identify orders with pending or due payments.
  • Use payment information for reconciliation and cashier review.

Typical workflow

  1. Open the Payments menu.
  2. Review the list of payments.
  3. Use filters or search to find a specific order or payment record.
  4. Check due amounts where an order has not been fully paid.

Good practice

  • Reconcile payment records with cash drawer, mobile money, card, or bank records at the end of each shift.
  • Follow up due payments promptly.
  • Train cashiers to record the correct payment method.
