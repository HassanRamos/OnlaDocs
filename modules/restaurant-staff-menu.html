Restaurant Staff Menu

Add restaurant staff members and assign roles so each person has the right level of access.

What this module is forMain featuresTypical workflowGood practiceScreens / Visual Guide

What this module is for

  • The staff module is used to manage employees who access the restaurant system.
  • Staff may include admins, managers, waiters, cashiers, kitchen users, or other team members based on your configuration.
  • Roles help control what each staff member can view or do.

Main features

  • Add new staff members.
  • Edit staff details.
  • Assign or change staff roles.
  • Control access through role and permission settings.

Typical workflow

  1. Open the Staff menu.
  2. Click Add Member.
  3. Enter the staff member’s details.
  4. Choose the correct role for that user.
  5. Save the record and confirm that the staff member appears in the list.

Good practice

  • Give staff only the access they need for their job.
  • Deactivate or update users when staff leave the restaurant.
  • Review roles regularly, especially for cashier, manager, and admin permissions.
  • Use separate user accounts instead of sharing one login.
