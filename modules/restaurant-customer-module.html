Restaurant Customer Module

Keep customer records and view order history linked to each customer.

What this module is forMain featuresTypical workflowGood practiceScreens / Visual Guide

What this module is for

  • The customer module stores customer information for restaurants that need customer profiles, repeat ordering, or order history.
  • It helps the business identify previous customers and view the orders connected to them.

Main features

  • View customer records in one place.
  • See customer details and related order information.
  • Open a specific customer’s order list.
  • Use customer history for follow-up, loyalty, support, or dispute handling.

Typical workflow

  1. Open the Customer module.
  2. Search or filter for the customer.
  3. Open the customer record to view details.
  4. Click the order-related action to view that customer’s orders.

Good practice

  • Avoid creating duplicate customers with slightly different names or phone numbers.
  • Keep customer contact details accurate.
  • Use customer order history when handling complaints, repeat orders, or loyalty discussions.
