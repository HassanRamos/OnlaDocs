Quick Start Guide

Use this checklist when setting up a new restaurant account or training a new client. Complete the setup steps before the restaurant starts recording real orders.

Initial SetupDaily OperationsEnd-of-Day ClosingStaff Training

Initial setup checklist

  1. Configure restaurant profile.
    Set business name, phone number, country, timezone, currency, logo, and theme from Settings.
  2. Create branches and areas.
    Add branches where applicable, then create areas such as main dining, lounge, garden, bar, or VIP.
  3. Add tables and QR codes.
    Create tables with seating capacity and print/test the QR codes if customers will self-order.
  4. Build the menu.
    Create menus, categories, and menu items. Confirm prices, item availability, and category grouping.
  5. Add staff and roles.
    Create staff accounts and assign the correct roles for admins, managers, waiters, kitchen users, and cashiers.
  6. Set taxes and payment options.
    Configure taxes and payment gateways/methods before taking real orders.
  7. Test the POS flow.
    Create a test order, send KOT, bill the order, record payment, and verify reports.

Daily operations flow

  • Open POS and confirm tables are available before service starts.
  • Take orders through POS or QR ordering depending on the restaurant setup.
  • Send KOTs to the kitchen and update KOT status when food is ready or cancelled.
  • Generate the bill and collect payment before closing the order.
  • Use the order and payment screens to confirm that no order has been missed.

End-of-day closing

  • Review open orders and close completed orders.
  • Check payments by method and compare with actual cash/mobile/card totals.
  • Review due payments and outstanding balances.
  • Open reports to confirm sales totals, top items, and category performance.
  • Check shift-based totals if operational shifts are enabled.
